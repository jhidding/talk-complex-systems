What is a complex system?

  • Simple microscopic rules of interaction

  • Emergent large scale behaviour:

    • scaling laws
    • pattern formation
    • synchronisation
    • self-replication
    • co-evolution
    • non-linear / chaotic
    • robustness
    • path-dependence
